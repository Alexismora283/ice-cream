Strawberry Ice Cream with White Chocolate

Why is it so special?

Strawberry ice cream with white chocolate combines the freshness of strawberries with the creamy sweetness of white chocolate, creating a perfectly balanced flavor.

Fun Facts

  • This flavor is popular at celebrations for its elegance and sweetness.
  • It pairs wonderfully with toppings like almonds and berries.
  • White chocolate adds an extra creamy texture.

Nutritional Information

Per 100 grams of ice cream:

  • Calories: 220
  • Fat: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 25g
  • Protein: 3g
